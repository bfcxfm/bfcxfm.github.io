About

Fangming Du is a Singapore and Austria trained architectural graduate. Before working in Singapore, he has worked and studied in Europe as well as China.

Fangming has acquired a vast experience working with multiple digital design platforms, using Rhino (Grasshopper, Vray etc.), Revit and other related BIM software during his years of practice. He has a great passion in multi-discipline design with emerging technologies.

Since 2014, Fangming has be working on the design and coordination of some of the large scale transportation projects in Singapore, including the world's first four in one bus and rail depot.
