Du Fangming | Architect

Trained in Singapore and Austria, Fangming Du holds a Master Degree from University of Singapore, he is a registered Architect in Singapore and Chartered Member of RIBA. He has been involved in a wide continuum of architectural projects comprising transportation, master planning, residential, institutional and commercial buildings. His passion lies in solving issues with computational design and emerging technologies. In 2016, he set up [Creature Atelier] for academic and research purpose. He has practiced in MKPL, and Hassell in which he was the local representative head of computation design, Singapore.
His current role is the Project Architect in Populous, involving in some of the celebrated projects across the globe.

| Kai Tak Sports Park, Hong Kong - Urban Oasis which integrates high-quality sports venues with retail, wellness, recreational and community spaces. © Kai Tak Sports Park
